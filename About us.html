About us

This knowledge & trainings collection performed by the Boeing Design Center in Moscow. During the past 25 years of operation, BDC employees took part in all Boeing commercial programs and services. Having so, It was critical to keep engineering teams efforts to take basic, specific and advanced knowledge constantly to gain desirable skills, increase performance, improve quality and grow responsibility. Today, this unique collection is available to all international Boeing sites and engineering teams and structured by Boeing skill-code, disciplines and the airplane platforms.

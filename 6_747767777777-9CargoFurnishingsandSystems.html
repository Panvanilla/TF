Pre-Requisites

These instructions provide detailed steps on how to complete a Registry package, however basic knowledge of PDM, Enovia, CATIA is required. Students should have completed PDM, BPDS, ENOVIA and CATIA V5 trainings: TR009024 – CATIA v5 Drafting Workbench, ENG1480 Introduction to Define and Control Airplane Configuration - Product Data Manager (DCAC-PDM), TR008737 ENOVIA V5 Essentials, TR003187 CATIA V5/ENOVIA LCA Product Design Workshop, TR010881 Product Definition Data Workshop for V4-V5 Program, TR001114 CATIA V5 Foundations for Aerospace Designers, PSTMO101 Module and MLNA Basics, PSTMO102 Module Option Expression Basics, PSTMO103 Generate Configuration and Includes Authorization, PSTMO104 Module Invalidation, Protecting Part Demand and e!Vista Instruction Sets, PSTMO105 Production Exception Objects, Modules and Refires, Module Management. For BPDS (Boeing Product Development System) training minimal requirement is completion of the following web-based trainings: 77004 Product Standards Data System (PSDS)and Process Specifications, ME1001 Intro to Boeing Product Definition Data, ME1002 Airplane Coordinate System,s ME1003 Product Definition Data Fundamentals, ME1004 Product Definition Data Associated Eng, ME1005 Introduction to Model Based Definition Data, ME1006 Model Based Definition Data Integration. Completion of BDC BPDS training is preferable option. Recommented to complete Interiors Familiarization Training first.

Overview

1st Part – Overview. Provides a thorough overview of Cargo Furnishings and Systems commodities. Gives basic knowledge of design features of Main Deck and Lower lobe cargo compartments as well as design requirements and objectives applicable to them. This part is aimed at newcomers and experienced employees who had not worked with Cargo Furnishings and Systems before. 2nd Part – Training plan, reference information and training activities to familiarize engineers with typical Cargo Furnishings and Systems products design including details, assemblies and installations. Student will create Typical CAD Code 5 and CAD Code 6 models and drawings using general descriptions, fulfill self-check and discuss questions and mistakes with instructor.

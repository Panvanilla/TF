Overview

The course consists of Overview, Training tasks and Instructions. 1st Part – Overview. Provides a quick overview of Airplane Painting Process and gives basic knowledge of main aspects, steps of the process and terminology. This part is aimed at newcomers and experienced employees who had not worked with Exterior Markings before. 2nd Part provides basic information on how to complete “Registry” packages for “747/767/777 Exterior Markings” Project as well as training tasks, and intended for use by both - newcomers and experienced engineers. 3rd Part - Instructions. It provides basic information on how to complete “Registry” packages for “787 Exterior Markings” Project and intended for use by both - newcomers and experienced engineers.

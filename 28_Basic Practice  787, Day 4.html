Overview

ME Basic Practice 787, Day 4. This part covers Sealing application process. Students will review the types of sealants, basic requirements and definitions, methods of application, review the main documents/standards for sealants and how to incorporate these requirements in Work instructions. By the end of this course student will be able to understand Sealing (purpose, types and application methods), identify sealant requirements from documents, identify and implement seal application requirements into Installation Plan, navigate Standard Operation Library (SOL) application. ME Basic Practice course provides a quick overview of Manufacturing Engineering roles and responsibilities, gives basic knowledge of main aspects, steps of ME processes and terminology. The course is divided into Days and must be taken in a certain sequence, each Day covers specific topic(s) and contains theoretical and practice part. Target audience is newcomers after BPDS and ME School completion or employees without relevant ME experience.

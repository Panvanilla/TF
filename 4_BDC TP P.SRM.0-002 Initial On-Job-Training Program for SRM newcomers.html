Overview

This training program created for preparation of Structural Repair Manual engineers for any SRM program support. Program provides familiarization with basic required processes and applications such as Enovia Essentials, IVT, BRIDGE, BoGART, List Builder, eMod, WSRD Overview, Aircraft Structural Repairs for Engineers (Part 1 and 2 - code 463), Simplified Technical English and also includes BDC TM P.SRM.0-001 SOJT for SRM Authors: «Structural Repair Manual Development» The purpose of the manual is to form general knowledge about the methods of collecting, analyzing and processing data on the design of an airplane, the production technology and assembly of components, the principles of an airplane operation for the subsequent creation of a SRM procedure ready for publication.

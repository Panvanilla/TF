Overview

Know requirements Tier-levels.
Know most common requirements-related terms such as “SCD”, “DOORS”, “Tracing”, “Allocation”, “GTR”, “Verification method”.
Understand definition of correct requirement.
Be familiar with requirements attributes and description of each attribute; be able to fill in attributes (including Object type, Verification method and other mandatory attributes).
Be able to complete tracing process for Tier-3 requirements to Tier-2 parents.
Know the process how to add, delete requirements or modify requirements text and attributes.

There are 4 tasks for student to test knowledge on the points above.

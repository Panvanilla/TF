Overview

Provides a plan of initial training program for engineers to start work as an AMM Authors. It applies to newcomers or engineers transferred from other projects/disciplines. The goal is to give the students common knowledge of AMM Authoring procedures, processes and tools. Program includes but not limited to: Authoring in eMOD, Toolbox, BCS, FrontMatter, Pageblock Numbering, ACTS, Data Customization, AMTOSS, List Management Request, Automated Front Matter, Reason for Change, Warnings and Cautions, Notes, References, Tools Consumables, Zones and Access Panels, Circuit Breakers Expendables, Applicability, WSRD, COC etc.

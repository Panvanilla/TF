EBAT ME SOW overview

Production Engineering

Pre-Requisites

Global Trade Awareness (80537); BPDS (77004, ME1001-1005); ME School (ME 103 Manufacturing Engineering Basics TR014644, ME 201 Installation Planning for ME TR015756); IP MES (CMES General Navigation TR016087, IP - MES Basics for BCA Manufacturing Engineering TR016091); ENOVIA V5 Essentials (TR008737); CATIA V5 Basic Concepts and Operation (TR001112); ME Basic Practice; ME Sealing Qualification (TR007452); MPQ-IP and Assessment (TR002411) and OJT 777 Systems trainings should be successfully completed before this course. At least 3-4 months of 777 Systems Manufacturing Engineering work experience is required.

Required Tools/Accounts

PDM, CAPP, CMES, REDARS, PSDS, Square

Student level

4

Basic duration

2 hours

SMC - Structures/Systems & Test/Assembly and Installation/Manufacturing Engineer-General

6H6G, 6H6H

Overview

This course gives basic knowledge of main aspects of EBAT tasks, steps of ME processes and terminology. By the end of this course student will be able to perform EBAT ME SOW. Target audience is newcomers who will support electrical ME team after BPDS, ME School, ME Basic Practice and OJT Systems completion.

Scope:

The training was created to introduce Boeing newcomers and all who may concern with Boeing main standards required for Systems workstatements, fasteners and finishes mostly used in Systems drawings with design limitations. Students will know which standards to pick up and how to choose right detail and assembly/installation finishes based on Boeing standards. Using Catia V5 and 787 approach student will have an assessment to create simple assembly requirement model and put the proper fasteners/finishes. Also sort of tests included.
